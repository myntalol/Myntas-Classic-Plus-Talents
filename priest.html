Patch Notes

Priest Racials

  • Troll Priest - Hex of Weakness reworked into Loa's Blessing:
    • Instantly heal a friendly target and increase their size and movement speed by 10% for 10 sec. 3 min cooldown.
  • Troll Priest - Shadowguard no longer costs mana or has charges.
  • Human Priest - Castigation, a new racial, is available to train at level 20:
    • Burns the target with light, causing Holy damage over 24 sec. While the target is burning, the priest's Spirit is increased by 5%.
      Shares a cooldown with Feedback.
  • Dwarf Priest - Hymn of the Mountain, a new racial, is available to train at level 20:
    • Creates a shield that absorbs Physical damage equal to 100% of the Priest's total Armor on all party members within 30 yds.
      Lasts 10 sec. 6 min cooldown.
  • Undead Priest - Touch of Weakness reworked:
    • Touch your target with a rotting, cursed hand, dealing Shadow damage and preventing their attacks from being able to critical strike you for 20 sec. 5 min cooldown.
  • Night Elf Priest - Elune’s Grace now increases spell casting speed by 20% instead of reducing ranged damage taken. Still provides 10% Dodge chance.
  • Night Elf Priest - Starshards mana cost reduced by 100%.
  • Fear Ward is now trainable for all Priests and is usable while in Shadowform.

    Note - Priest is one of the classes I play most, and I always wanted Blizzard to lean into the racials, not get rid of them. Like this whole project, I believe any "Classic+" that comes out should lean into RPG-inspired ideas and prioritize fun > balance, so I took a shot at tweaking the racials to be more unique and slightly more impactful.

Balance Changes

  • Silence global cooldown removed.
  • Mind Flay spell coefficient increased by approximately 30%.
  • Mind Flay no longer loses a tick of damage when the last tick is clipped by another Mind Flay cast.
  • Holy Nova now gains additional benefit from spell damage and healing bonuses.
  • Prayer of Healing now heals the target's party, rather than being limited to just your direct party.
  • Power Word: Shield low ranks of this spell are now penalized the same way healing spells are penalized.
  • Power Word: Shield damage absorption added by the caster's bonus healing has been increased to 20%.
  • Power Word: Shield will now get a bonus from items and effects which increase your healing.
  • Periodic heals (HoTs) cast by different players can now be active on the same target simultaneously.

New Spells and Abilities

  • Divine Spirit is now trainable at level 30:
    • Holy power infuses the target, increasing their Spirit for 30 min.
  • Shadow Word: Death is now trainable at level 35:
    • A word of dark binding that inflicts Shadow damage to the target. If the target is not killed by Shadow Word: Death, the caster takes damage equal to 40% of the damage attempted against the target. 12 sec cooldown.
  • Binding Heal is now trainable at Level 45:
    • Heals a friendly target and the caster. Generates a low amount of threat.
  • Shadowfiend is now trainable at level 60:
    • Creates a shadowy fiend to attack the target. Caster receives 3% mana when the Shadowfiend attacks. Lasts 15 sec.

Hero's Journey

    Upon reaching level 60, the Priest discovers the ability to /commune with other Priests to temporarily exchange racial abilities for 3 hours.
    • Note: There may be beings outside the playable races that are willing to exchange racial powers...
