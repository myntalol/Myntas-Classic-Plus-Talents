Note from the creator

Hi there! I created this site from scratch over 12 months following Season of Discovery. As an avid WoW player since 2009, Vanilla & TBC are my favorite eras of the game. Classic+ rumors inspired me to get creative and make my own class revamps that are grounded in an old school RPG world. While I love Vanilla, I believe any "Classic+" that may or may not release should do class revamps, not just add content like new raids. I know some people disagree with that, and that's ok! Baldur's Gate 3 and Diablo 4 inspired me a lot during this time, so I incorporated ideas from those games into the talents and Feats (which are similar to Runes in SoD).

TLDR: I made this to explore Classic+ ideas and wanted to share it with the community, feel free to let me know what you think!
- Mynta, GM of Aggrophobia, fear bombing mobs since 2006, Andorhal PvP | NA (@mynlol on Discord)
