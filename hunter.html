Patch Notes

Balance Changes

  • Prowess - Hunters no longer use mana. Their abilities instead generate Prowess when successfully damaging or crowd-controlling enemies. Upon reaching 100 Prowess, the Hunter gains 10% increased attack speed, diminishing back down to 0 over 10 sec.
      Note - Instead of Focus from Cata, I thought this iteration of a non-mana resource would differentiate Hunters and align thematically RPG-wise. Blizzard stated their idea was always to have Hunters not use mana, they simply ran out of time in Vanilla.
  • Glaives is now trainable as a baseline skill at level 20.
  • Ranged attack minimum range has been reduced to 5 yards.
  • Distracting Shot now taunts the target for 3 seconds and only has one rank.
  • Wingclip no longer does damage.
  • Mongoose Bite no longer requires the Hunter to Dodge.
  • Traps can now be set while in combat and require a 1.5 second arming time.
  • Auto shot will automatically enable if the Hunter steps out of melee range while in combat with the target.
  • Auto shot is now reset when casting Aimed Shot.
  • Scorpid Sting now reduces the target's chance to hit with melee and ranged attacks by 3% for 20 sec.
  • Pets now have their own talent trees and can learn talents in one of three trees depending on their creature family. Pets gain talent points starting at level 20 and earn an extra talent point every 4 levels.
  • Pet families now have one unique ability. New abilities have been added for families such as bears.
  • Stable masters can now be visited to recover lost pets. Stable masters can now also accommodate two additional pets.
  • Disengage has been reworked:
    • The hunter leaps backward 13 yds and reduces their total threat by 5%. 45 sec cooldown.
  • Rapid Fire has been updated into Rapid Killing:
    • Increases ranged and melee attack speed by 30% for 12 sec. 5 min cooldown.

New Spells and Abilities

  • Steady Shot is now trainable at level 30:
    • A steady shot that causes base ranged weapon damage.
  • Silencing Shot is now trainable at level 60:
    • A shot that deals 20% ranged weapon damage and Silences the target for 3 sec. 45 sec cooldown.
  • Misdirection is now trainable at level 50:
    • Causes a friendly target to receive the threat generated by your next damaging attack and all actions taken for 3 sec afterwards.
      1 min cooldown.

Hero's Journey

    Upon reaching level 60, the Hunter undergoes field training and chooses from one of three traits. This choice is permanent and cannot be undone:
    • Efficient Packing - Your arrows and bullets stack up to 220 per bag slot.
    • Bonded Pair - Mend Pet increases your pet's happiness slightly.
    • Honed Instincts - Reduces the cooldown of Feign Death by 5 sec.
