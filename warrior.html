Patch Notes

Balance Changes

  • Rage Generation has been updated to The Burning Crusade formula.
    • Each auto-attack will provide a set amount of Rage, with off-hand weapons granting 50% of the Rage main-hand weapons do. Rage from damage taken will no longer be based on a standard creature of the character's level, but instead will be based on the health of the warrior or druid. This calculation ignores all damage reduction from armor, absorption, avoidance, block, or similar mechanics, so improving your gear will not reduce Rage gained.

      Note - Yes it's true...I am a Warrior main advocating for Rage normalization. I know many would hate this for Classic+, but I understand why Blizz did this back in TBC. For tanks, better gear = worse Rage? Awful. The problem wasn't "Warriors spam abilities too much", so I made talents inspired by SoD like Flagellation & improved Unbridled Wrath / Anger Management so you can always zug zug.
  • Glaives is now trainable as a baseline skill at level 20.
  • Revenge and Overpower no longer share a cooldown.
  • Charge is now usable in combat.
  • Charge and Intervene stun effect is no longer subject to diminishing returns.
  • Charge, Intercept and Intervene now suppress all Slowing effects while charging the target.
  • Battle Stance now increases the Warrior's chance to hit by 3%.
  • Whirlwind now strikes with both weapons while the Warrior is dual-wielding.
  • Slam now suspends the weapon swing timer rather than resetting it.
  • Recklessness, Retaliation and Shield Wall cooldowns are no longer linked to each other.

New Spells and Abilities

  • Spell Reflection is now trainable at level 50:
    • Raise your shield, reflecting the next spell cast on you. Lasts 5 sec. 25 Rage. 10 sec cooldown,
  • Intervene is now trainable at level 60:
    • Run at high speed towards a party or raid member, intercepting the next melee or ranged attack made against them as well as reducing their total threat. 10 Rage. 30 sec cooldown.
  • Commanding Shout is now trainable at level 30:
    • Increases maximum health of all party members within 20 yards. Lasts 2 min. 10 Rage.

Hero's Journey

    Upon reaching level 60, the Warrior chooses from one of three traits. This choice is permanent and cannot be undone:
    • Giant's Strength - Your backpack gains 12 additional slots.
    • Tactical Soldier - The healing provided by your bandages and the duration of food buffs are increased by 25%.
    • Knightly Charisma - The cost of repairing items is reduced by 20%.
