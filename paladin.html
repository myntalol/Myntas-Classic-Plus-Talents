Patch Notes

Balance Changes

  • Blessing of Kings, Wisdom, Salvation, Might and Light have had their durations increased by 100%.
  • Seal of Command no longer has a chance to partially resist.
  • Consecration bonus damage coefficient has been increased on all ranks.
  • Exorcism can now be cast on any target and has increased critical strike chance on Undead and Demons.
  • Holy Wrath can now be cast on any target and stuns Undead and Demons for up to 2 sec.
  • Lay on Hands cooldown reduced to 20 min.
  • Hammer of Wrath is now a Retribution spell (was Holy) and scales with attack power and spell damage.
  • Hammer of Wrath mana cost reduced, missile speed increased, now usable on targets below 35% health.
  • Hammer of Wrath now suspends the weapon swing timer rather than resetting it.
      Note - "The #1 Ret Paladin in the world believes the light should be easier to swing." - Esfand.
  • Judgement of Justice now also limits the target's maximum run speed for the duration.
  • Righteous Fury now increases threat caused by Holy damage by 90% (was 60%).
  • Blessing of Freedom cooldown increased by 5 sec.
  • Divine Protection has been reworked:
    • Reduces damage taken by 10% and increases healing received by 10% for 10 sec. 1 min cooldown.

New Spells and Abilities

  • Hand of Reckoning is now trainable at Level 30:
    • Taunts the target to attack you, but has no effect if the target is already attacking you. 10 sec cooldown.
  • Consecration is now trainable at level 20.
    • Consecrates the land beneath you, doing Holy damage over 8 sec to enemies who enter the area. 8 sec cooldown.
  • Divine Illumination is now trainable at Level 60:
    • Reduces the mana cost of all spells by 30% for 15 sec. 5 min cooldown.
  • Avenging Wrath is now trainable at level 50:
    • Increases all damage and healing caused by 20% for 20 sec. Causes Forbearance. 3 min cooldown.

Hero's Journey

    Upon reaching level 60, the Paladin chooses from one of three traits. This choice is permanent and cannot be undone:
    • Light's Grace - Cleanse and Purify cost 10% less mana.
    • Light's Tenacity - Increases your Hammer of Justice range by 5 yards.
    • Light's Calling - Divine Illumination reduces your damage taken by 3%.
